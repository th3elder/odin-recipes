Grilled sea bass

This is one plain, fast, delicious and really easy recipe. But you get everything you need for building muscles.

Tons of minerals and most important omega-3 fat oils.

Plenty of protein, almost none of fats, and some vitamins too. Enjoy!

    List of ingredients: (for 1 person)

  • 200g of sea bass fillet
  • 100g of brown or white rice
  • 1tbsp of olive oil
  • 1/4 lemon
  • spices
    I reccomend himalayan salt, peppercorns, rosemary, lemon juice. Vegeta, turmeric or curry for rice.

Meal preparation:

  1. Put 100g of rice in cooking pot, double the water of rice amount. exmp. 1 cup of rice=2 cups of water
  2. Salt it
  3. Season your fish. I put peppercorns, himalayan salt and rosemary. You can do this few hour earlier, it will taste better
  4. Heat pan with 1tbsp of olive oil in it and put fish to cook. You cook skin side first.
  5. Cook it on medium fire, 3-5minutes and turn it on other side. Skin should be a little bit chrispy by now.
  6. Cook it for 5 more minutes max.
  7. Rice should be almost done by now, 3 minutes before its over, put seasoning in rice. I prefer turmeric and vegeta (season mix).
  8. Boil it untill there's no water.
  9. Serve the fish and rice on plate, and squeese 1/4 of lemon on fish if you want.
  10. Enjoy your meal!

Macros:

  • 723kcal
  • 55g of protein
  • 80g of carbohydrates
  • 20g of fats
